Deep Learning Course

I have created a DL Course on Udemy to take you from zero to hero. This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of deep learning techniques and their applications. Throughout this course, you will learn various concepts, tools, and practical skills related to deep learning.

The following curriculum covers a wide range of topics and progresses from beginner to intermediate and advanced levels. Whether you are new to deep learning or have some experience, this course will provide you with the knowledge and skills to excel in the field.
